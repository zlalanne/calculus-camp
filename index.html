Getting Ready For Calculus Camp

The AP College Board describes AP Calculus AB as a course that "is designed to develop mathematical knowledge concepeturally, guiding you to connect topics and representations throughout the course and to apply strategies and techniques to accurately solve diverse types of problems."

Getting Ready for Calculus Camp will review the skills and academic vocabulary you NEED to be set up for success in calculus and preview techniques you will see in calculus.

What can I expect to get out of this?

  • Review essential skills necessary for calculus concepts
  • Formative assessment for each topic
  • Cornell style notes to increase retention & practice note-taking skills
  • Review packet to enable refreshing of topics throughout the year
  • Cumulative data summary of level of mastery for covered skills
